# Candidate Campaign Audit

## Release correction

A direct visual review found that the first candidate vision was technically complete but did not meet the required standard for perceived craft, visual authority, or a contemporary 2026 executive experience.

The site was rebuilt rather than cosmetically patched. The prior flat card presentation was replaced by a Wolfe-specific Trust Issuance Theater, premium Data Use Card material treatment, stronger full-page pacing, a more immersive dark/light/orange composition, and a three-part interactive issuance studio.
