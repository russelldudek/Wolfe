04 / THE COMPANY BEHAVIOR

Governance should feel like product infrastructure, not committee infrastructure.

01NAME

Give every priority dataset a stable identity.

Authoritative source, business meaning, lineage, sensitivity, and owner become discoverable by default.

02RATE

Quantify trust instead of implying it.

Quality dimensions, thresholds, coverage, freshness, and known limitations become visible and comparable.

03BOUND

Attach approved uses and retained authority.

The card states where AI may assist, where it must abstain, and which humans retain decision rights.

04SERVE

Make remediation a service with an owner.

Detection, routing, escalation, response expectations, and closure evidence are built into the platform.

05LEARN

Turn every exception into a better standard.

Usage, questions, quality failures, and outcomes feed the next version of the data product and its terms.
