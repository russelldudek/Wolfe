## Typography evidence and decision
No authoritative public brand guide or licensed font package was located. Wolfe's current public pages exhibit a compact geometric/humanist sans-serif character with heavy headlines, direct utility copy, and sparse technical labels.

The campaign uses open-source Manrope for display and body typography, IBM Plex Mono for technical labels, and locally available Lato/system sans fallbacks. The fonts are loaded from Google Fonts under their open-source licenses; no proprietary font binaries are copied into the repository.

## Public visual grammar
- Charcoal and white dominate, with orange used decisively rather than diffusely.
- Strong, compressed headline hierarchy and direct section language.
- Clean white space contrasted with dark product and solution surfaces.
- Gift-card, issuance, platform, merchant, fulfillment, and personalization cues.
- Crisp rectangular rails and production geometry rather than repetitive rounded-card grids.

## Role-derived visual concept
The redesigned candidate vision uses a **Trust Issuance Theater**. Multiple source streams enter an issuance rig and emerge as one governed Data Use Card carrying explicit quality, meaning, ownership, approved-use, authority, and service terms.

The visual idea is grounded in Wolfe's public card-personalization and same-day fulfillment character without pretending to depict an internal Wolfe system. It translates the role's actual work into a company-specific argument: data should not merely arrive; it should be issued for use with verifiable terms.

## Signature motion and interaction
- Hero: a one-shot card issuance sequence, scanner pass, verification gates, and stable ready state.
- Pointer-capable desktop: restrained perspective response on the issuance scene.
- Issuance Studio: four illustrative dataset contexts atomically update the card, posture, ownership, approved use, human authority, restriction, evidence, color state, ARIA state, and one-shot scanner motion.
- Rapid selection: the final request remains authoritative.
- Reduced motion: all information resolves immediately to a complete stable state.

## Artifact use
- Website: full charcoal/orange expression, official Wolfe identity above the fold, oversized issuance theater, open editorial pacing, and interactive Data Use Card studio.
- Resume and cover letter: restrained official logo, charcoal rules, orange section markers, and visible campaign URL.
- Interview brief, 120-day plan, and Data Use Card standard: same logo, typography, color, rectangular rules, and contract geometry.
